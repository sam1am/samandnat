Transportation to Villa

On April 5th, we'll provide transportation from Athens to Villa Delenia. The bus will depart from Central Athens Hotel at 10:00 AM. The journey takes approximately 2.5 hours.

Packing Tips

April in Greece can be mild to warm during the day (60-70°F) and cooler in the evenings (50-60°F). We recommend bringing:

  • Layers for varying temperatures
  • Comfortable walking shoes for Athens
  • Formal attire for the wedding ceremony
  • Swimwear (the villa has a pool)
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses

What's Included

We are thrilled to host you at Villa Delenia! We'll be covering the cost of your accommodation, the wedding dinner, and some house snacks and drinks during the villa stay (April 5-9). Group transportation to/from Athens will be provided on April 5 and April 9.

One shared meal will be prepared each day with costs split among guests. You're welcome to bring additional drinks and snacks to share!

Guests are responsible for their own flights to Athens, accommodations before the villa stay (April 3-4), and any meals outside of the villa events.
